Achievements

Among my life's achievements I am most proud of being a responsible and caring father. However, before I started my family, I managed to accomplish a life dream - a trip arround the world. In 2016, My wife and I set out on a year-long backpacking worldwide trip. The goal was to make a lap around the globe, and to see and experience as much as possible in the process. We went from Iceland to Canada, Texas US, Ecuadoor, Peru, Bolivia, Panama, Costarica, Florida, Nevada, California, Hawaii, New Zealand, Australia, Japan, Malesia, Vietnam, Cambodia and Tailand.

Travel and volunteering

In my opinion, while traveling, there is no better way to meet the locals, interact with them and experience their everyday life than volunteering! There are all sorts of things that you can do, from helping local business owners with their websites to cleaning horse stables or working on a rice field. Volunteering challenges you to try new things but can also impact local communities, especially in developing countries.

TV, press and radio

I wrote multiple articles for local and national papers and organized meetings for people interested in traveling where my wife and I could share our experiences and talk about our journey. We have also been invited to a live morning tv program and various radio shows. In 2019 we were regular guests on a radio program about budget traveling.

Free code camps for children

In 2022, as part of my cooperation with the local library, I starting runing free programing lessons for children aged 7-9. Students learned how to construct complex solutions using basic programing commands such as loops and conditionals. We used the Scratch programing language and an interactive robot called Photon. Robots were programed with computer commands and their code was used to control their movements, interact with their surroundings and solve logical problems.

The first group of students has just completed the course and received their diplomas and gifts.
